BGP Incident:
Summary:AS7007 announces many specific prefixes
Start:1997-04-25 11:30:00
End:Unknown
Details:

At about 11:30am (this is when we saw it), they started redistributing more specifics for many (thousands) of CIDR routes to Sprintlink [1239 1790] but the routes were announced - AND the routes were injected with an origin AS of 7007, hiding the AS_PATH the routes had (and the customer involved).
